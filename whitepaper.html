The cost of losing the people who know where to look first.

Plant-floor judgment is a strategic asset. When experienced troubleshooting knowledge stays informal, recurring problems survive and capability becomes dependent on a few people.

Why recurring problems survive.

Manufacturing operations often restore production quickly while leaving the actual condition hidden between maintenance, controls, process, instrumentation, and operations. AISA focuses on that gap.
